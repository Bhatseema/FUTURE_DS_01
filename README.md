📊 Data Analytics Dashboard Project

🚀 Project Overview

This project showcases three interactive dashboards built using **Power BI** to analyze different business domains:

* Customer Churn Analytics
* Marketing Funnel Performance
* Retail Sales Analysis

The goal of this project is to extract meaningful insights, track KPIs, and support data-driven decision-making.

📌 1. Customer Churn Analytics Dashboard

🎯 Objective

To analyze customer behavior and identify key factors contributing to customer churn.

📊 Key KPIs

* Total Customers: 4000
* Churn Rate: 49.30%
* Customer Lifetime Value (CLV): 2.53K
* Churned Customers: 1972
* Avg Monthly Charges: 69.91

📈 Key Insights

* Customers with month-to-month contracts have higher churn
* Fiber optic users show higher churn rates
* Certain payment methods influence churn behavior
* Customers without tech support are more likely to churn

📊 Visualizations Used

* Bar Charts (Contract, Payment Method)
* Pie Chart (Churn Distribution)
* Line Chart (Tenure vs Churn)
* KPI Cards
* Slicers (Contract, Internet Service, Tenure)

📌 2. Marketing Funnel Performance Dashboard

🎯 Objective

To analyze the marketing funnel and track conversion performance across different channels.

📊 Key KPIs

* Total Leads: 786
* Customers: 317
* Revenue per Customer: 242.45K
* Final Conversion Rate: 0.40
* Total Revenue: 76.86M

📈 Key Insights

* Social Media generates highest customer conversions
* Significant drop-off observed between funnel stage
* Campaign A performs better than others
* Revenue contribution varies across stages

📊 Visualizations Used

* Funnel Chart (Lead Stages)
* Bar Chart (Channel Performance)
* Stacked Chart (Campaign Analysis)
* Pie Chart (Revenue by Stage)
* Treemap (Customers by Region)
* Slicers (Channel, Region, Device)


📌 3. Retail Sales Data Analysis Dashboard

🎯 Objective

To analyze sales performance across products, regions, and time.

📊 Key KPIs

* Total Products: 200
* Total Sales: 23M
* Monthly Sales: 23M
* Avg Order Value: 117.37K

📈 Key Insights

* North region generates highest sales
* Certain product categories dominate revenue
* Monthly sales show fluctuating trends
* Top-selling products contribute major revenue share

📊 Visualizations Used

* Line Chart (Monthly Sales Trend)
* Bar Chart (Sales by Category)
* Map Visualization (Sales by City)
* Treemap (Sales by Product)
* Pie Chart (Sales by Subcategory)
* Slicers (Date, Category, Region, City)
